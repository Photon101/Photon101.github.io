# Image Integrity Triage Report

- Review metadata consistency against the supplied timeline and source-device facts
- Note JPEG/PNG compression, dimensions, edit-history clues, and obvious metadata gaps
- Inspect visible object, shadow, edge, lighting, perspective, and duplication anomalies
- Compare against supplied reference images when available
- Separate observations from confidence level and limitations
- Provide next evidence steps, such as requesting original files, comparison photos, device export, or specialist forensic review
