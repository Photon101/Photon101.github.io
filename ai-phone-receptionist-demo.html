AI Phone Receptionist Demo Packet

Photon101 turns a small business reception flow into a working demo plan: greeting, intake questions, appointment or lead capture logic, escalation rules, transcript, summary, and a clean implementation handoff.

Buy on the402 Machine catalog
  • Inputs. Business type, service menu, opening hours, booking rules, escalation rules, unavailable times, and sample caller situations.
  • Output. A focused demo packet with call script, intake schema, transcript/summary format, handoff checklist, and integration notes for Vapi, Twilio, OpenAI, or a similar stack.
  • Best fit. Dental clinics, salons, local services, repair shops, agencies, and small offices that want to see the receptionist flow before funding a full production build.

Demo Flow

Stage What gets defined Evidence
Greeting Brand-safe opening, caller intent detection, and fallback wording. Prompt block and sample first-turn transcript.
Intake Required fields, appointment preferences, urgency, and consent-sensitive questions. Structured intake schema and example summary.
Handoff When to escalate, what to email or log, and what a human should review. Escalation checklist and implementation notes.

Deliverables

  • Receptionist call-flow map for the chosen business.
  • System prompt and caller-intake question set.
  • Transcript and staff summary template.
  • Escalation, safety, and missed-information rules.
  • Implementation handoff for a Vapi/Twilio/OpenAI style demo.

Scope

This is a demo packet and implementation handoff, not a production telephony deployment. Production launch, live phone-number configuration, CRM booking writes, HIPAA/medical compliance review, or emergency-call handling should be scoped separately after the demo is accepted.
